### Quick Notes
- **Most popular for open-source/developer docs** — Docusaurus, MkDocs (Material), and VitePress/Docsify lead in adoption.
- **Performance kings** — VitePress, Hugo, and Astro/Starlight (Vite/Hugo/Astro deliver near-instant dev servers and fast production builds).
- **Markdown-first** — All support Markdown heavily; most allow MDX or components for interactivity.
- **Choosing one**:
  - Vue/JS team → **VitePress** (fast & modern) or VuePress.
  - React/TS team → **Docusaurus** or **Nextra**.
  - Python project → **MkDocs** or **Sphinx**.
  - Maximum speed/large sites → **Hugo** or **Astro Starlight**.
  - Zero build/runtime simplicity → **Docsify**.
